## Exam Information

### Format

- **Total Questions**: 60 per exam
- **Time Limit**: 90 minutes
- **Question Type**: Multiple choice (single correct answer)
- **Passing Score**: 75% (45 out of 60 correct answers)
- **Open Book**: No (exam simulates actual conditions)

### Domain Weight Distribution

Questions are distributed according to official KCNA exam weights:

| Domain | Weight | Questions per Exam |

## Tips

### Before the Exam

1. **Complete both mock exams** and score 75%+ consistently
2. **Review wrong answers** thoroughly, understand why they're wrong
3. **Practice kubectl commands** - many questions reference them


### During the Exam

1. **Read questions carefully** - scenario-based questions test application of knowledge
2. **Watch for keywords**: "always", "never", "only", "primary", "main"
3. **Flag and review** difficult questions at the end
4. **Trust your preparation** - first instinct is often correct
